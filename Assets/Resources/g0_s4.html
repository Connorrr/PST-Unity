During this Program, you will play games where you will see pictures on the screen all at the same time,
like the ones shown below. These are called "Picture Panels".
Some pictures will be GOOD, some pictures will be CALM, and some will be a bit unpleasant and scary.

In some games, you will be asked to click on the first GOOD picture you see.
For example, in the Picture Panel shown below, there are three GOOD pictures amongst the other pictures.
These include the cute rabbit, the smiling girl, and the happy children.
Just one of these GOOD pictures needs to be clicked on,
as shown below by the white arrow (mouse cursor) on the smiling girl.

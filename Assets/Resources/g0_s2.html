The third skill is learning to use BOTH OPTIONS of
looking for GOOD things and CALM things in every situation.

This means that looking for GOOD things is your first OPTION to try.
If there isn't something good to focus on,
then try your next OPTION which is to look for CALM things instead.

In everyday situations, we want you to use BOTH OPTIONS of
looking for GOOD things and CALM things
so you stay happy and calm!
